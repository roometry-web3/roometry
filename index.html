About ROOMETRY

ROOMETRY is a visual NFT showcase presenting a growing and limitless collection of isometric rooms—each crafted with unique artistic vision.

This evolving series is created by ETMFT, a digital artist dedicated to designing immersive spaces that spark imagination and curiosity.

Unlike traditional collections with fixed themes or quantities, ROOMETRY has no predefined limit. It is an ever-expanding universe of digital rooms, where each NFT introduces a completely different mood, vibe, or concept. From minimalistic elegance to surreal complexity, ROOMETRY embraces creative freedom without stylistic boundaries.

The project is still in active development, with new rooms being added regularly. Every NFT stands alone as a unique piece, but together they reflect the evolving mind of their creator ''ETMFT'' who treats each virtual space as a canvas for storytelling, emotion, and artistic experimentation.

ROOMETRY is more than a collection.
It’s a growing digital world. A gallery of possibilities. A journey through space reimagined.
